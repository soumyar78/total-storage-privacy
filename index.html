Total Storage

File Manager & Cloud Integration

1. Introduction

Welcome to Total Storage. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. Total Storage is a file manager application designed to help you organize your local files and manage your cloud storage efficiently.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

This policy applies specifically to the Android application published under the name Total Storage on the Google Play Store.

2. Google Drive Access

Total Storage integrates with Google Drive to provide seamless file management capabilities. Our application requests access to your Google Drive files only upon your explicit consent and interaction. We access your Google Drive data solely to enable you to:

  • Browse: View your folders and files within the app interface.
  • Upload: Upload local files from your device to your Google Drive.
  • Download: Save files from Google Drive to your local device.
  • Delete: Remove files or folders from Google Drive, only when you explicitly choose to do so.

3. Data Usage

  • No External Servers: Google Drive files are accessed directly between your device and Google’s servers. We do not store or proxy your files through any external servers.
  • No Background Access: The app does not access Google Drive data without your knowledge or action.
  • No Data Mining: We do not analyze or scan file contents for advertising, analytics, or profiling purposes.
  • User-Initiated Only: All file operations are performed strictly based on user actions.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Secure Token Storage: OAuth 2.0 access and refresh tokens are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage mechanisms.
  • Encrypted Communication: All interactions with Google Drive APIs occur over secure HTTPS connections.
  • Local-First Design: File metadata is cached locally for performance and is not accessible by other apps.

5. Data Sharing

  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell, trade, or rent user data.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share Google Drive data with advertisers, analytics providers, or external parties.
  • No Tracking: Google Drive data is never used for tracking or behavioral advertising.

6. User Control

  • Disconnect Anytime: You can remove your Google Drive account from the app at any time, which deletes stored tokens from your device.
  • Revoke Access: You can revoke access via your Google Account Permissions page.

7. Third-Party Services & Compliance

“The use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.”

Total Storage requests Google Drive access scopes only to enable core file management features such as browsing, uploading, downloading, and deleting files. These permissions are essential for the app’s primary functionality and are never used for advertising, analytics, or data profiling.

8. Children’s Privacy

Total Storage is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
